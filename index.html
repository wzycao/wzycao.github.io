Welcome to BGRB!

also known as... Blue = Good, Red = Bad.

Fly around as Temu Reimu while avoiding bullets and collecting powerups!

Get the higest score you can!

This game is loosely inspired from the Touhou Project series.

The twist is that it's 3d instead of 2d, as the games are traditionally.

Grazing works similarly, but the player hitboxes are a little wonky.

Additionally, movement isnt as razor-tight because I think it looks better smooth lol.

Note: you currently can't change controls :)
